# TSBA Director of Schools Search: Typical Finalist Counts

**Research Date:** 2026-03-17

## Question
How many candidates does TSBA typically recommend as finalists for director of schools searches? Greeneville City Schools and Campbell County both received only 2 finalists.

## Finding
TSBA's standard practice is to recommend **3 finalists** to school boards. Two finalists is unusually low and appears to be a recent deviation from the norm.

## Evidence: Finalist Counts by District

| District | Year | Applicants | TSBA-Recommended Finalists |
|---|---|---|---|
| Washington County | 2021 | 16 | **5** |
| Blount County | 2022 | unknown | **5** |
| Murfreesboro City | 2021 | unknown | **5** |
| Clarksville-Montgomery (CMCSS) | 2022 | unknown | **4** |
| Humboldt City (Gibson Co.) | 2021 | unknown | **4** |
| Rutherford County | 2018 | 15 | **4** |
| Knox County | 2022 | 18+ | **3** |
| Rutherford County | 2022 | 7 | **3** |
| Kingsport | 2023 | unknown | **3** (planned) |
| Memphis-Shelby County | 2023 | unknown | **3** |
| Coffee County | 2025 | 16 | **2** |
| Campbell County | 2026 | 15 | **2** |
| Greeneville City | 2026 | 10 | **2** |
| Sumner County | 2023 | 16 | **1** |

## Key Findings

1. **Three is the standard target.** In the Kingsport search, TSBA Executive Director Tammy Grissom explicitly laid out a timeline for presenting "the three finalists recommended by TSBA."

2. **Most districts received 3-5 finalists.** Of the 14 searches with data, 10 districts (71%) received 3 or more finalists. The median is 3-4.

3. **Two finalists is rare and recent.** The only districts receiving just 2 finalists from TSBA are Coffee County (2025), Campbell County (2026), and Greeneville (2026), all very recent searches.

4. **Campbell County saw it as a problem.** The board voted to add a third candidate (interim director Nancy Lay) after TSBA only returned two, which triggered public backlash about process integrity. Dr. Robert Angel publicly criticized the deviation.

5. **Applicant pool size doesn't explain it.** Campbell County had 15 applicants and got 2 finalists. Coffee County had 16 applicants and got 2. Meanwhile, Rutherford County had only 7 applicants in 2022 and still got 3 finalists.
